hydra the kernel of multiprocessor operating system this paper describes the design philosophy of hydra-the kernel of an operating system for mmp the carnegie-mellon multi-mini-processor this philosophy is realized through the introduction of generalized notion of resource both physical and virtual called an object mechanisms are presented for dealing with objects including the creation of new types specification of new operations applicable to given type sharing and protection of any reference to given object against improper application of any of the operations defined with respect to that type of object the mechanisms provide coherent basis for extension of the system in two directions the introduction of new facilities and the creation of highly secure systems cacm june 1974 wulf cohen corwin jones levin pierson pollack operating system kernel nucleus protection security 4.3 6.2 ca740614 jb january 17 1978 40 pm
